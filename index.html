Hi! My name is Adam and I thank you for visiting my portfolio site! I was born in Hungary and currently reside in San Diego, CA. Feel free to reach out or just say hello! I was born and raised in Hungary, then liven in London for a long time. My wife and I moved to California in 2018.

I started out as a UX intern for Vodafone while still in college, following that I spent most of my career in project management.

I am a tech junkie and I love to spend my time singing and playing the guitar, cooking or driving. Looking for wonderful clients and colleagues from all over the world to collaborate because I appreciate teamwork. My passion for coding kicked off while I worked for Amazon and we needed a tool to improve the delivery dispatch process and get metrics real time... So I started learning JavaScript. The rest is history! As a developer, I provide full stack web development services such as:
  • Buliding websites and apps with Bootsrap, React etc
  • Updating older code
  • Using modern tools to make sure you're product is current and looks great
  • Data engineering
  • UX / UI design
